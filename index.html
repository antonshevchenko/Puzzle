About Puzzlegram

Puzzlegram is a game that combines the greatness of a sliding puzzle and the beauty of Instagram pictures. We have a generous offer of 4 photos that you can use as puzzles to solve.

To start playing, please choose a puzzle as indicated above. After choosing a puzzle, the full rules of the game will be stated on the left side of the puzzle itself, below the puzzle preview image. Have fun!
